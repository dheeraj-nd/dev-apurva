We're hiring!

Community Engagement Lead

Apurva.ai, housed in the EkStep Foundation, is an open innovation platform, created for public good to support the efforts of the change leaders in the development sector. With rapidly increasing complex social problems, solutions need to evolve and keep pace. Apurva enables change leaders with the collective wisdom of their network in order to help them resolve societal challenges more effectively.

As a Community Engagement Lead at Apurva.ai, you will be responsible to build and strengthen the Apurva Community.

From identifying organizations and networks and onboarding them on Apurva, and to working with them closely to ensure seamless adoption, consistent usage and retention. Such organizations would include NGOs, foundations, orchestrators, Governments and/or social enterprises from the Global South.

Responsibilities:

  • Identify the target organizations based on mutual fitment and craft go-to-market approaches that resonate with these organizations.

  • Engage with these organizations to validate needs and align appropriate Apurva product offerings.

  • Enable the onboarding of new organizations on Apurva.

  • Engage with new and existing organizations on Apurva, to make sure that there is a mutual value exchange.

  • Identify frictions and challenges, and bring back feedback to the product team.

  • Analyze and report key metrics to track impact and identify areas for improvement.

  • Build strategic partnerships that leverage the broader network to accelerate Apurva adoption.

Requirements:

  • Should come from the Development Sector with a desire to make a difference and be outcome driven.

  • 6-8 years of experience and a lot of passion to foster communities of users.

  • Prior experience and partnerships in Africa and South America would be a big plus.

  • Good domain expertise in 2 or more of the following: Education, Livelihood, Financial Inclusion, Health, and Climate.

  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, and the ability to collaborate with a diverse range of stakeholders.

Job Type:

  • Full-Time / Part-Time/Volunteers/ Consultants/Contract to hire.

  • The position is based out of Bangalore.


If you see a strong fit, feel deeply passionate about this role, and are excited about what exponential technologies like AI can do for the social sector, write to us at contact@apurva.ai with your resume, a blurb on why this role resonates with you and how do you think you can contribute in this role.
